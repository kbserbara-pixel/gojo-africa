The Problem

  • Trust deficit — duplicated, stale, and fraudulent listings; no way to verify a landlord or a photo.
  • Information blackout — zero visibility into safety, utilities reliability, or true cost of living at an address.
  • Dumb search — keyword filters, no understanding of budget reality or lifestyle fit, no explanation of results.
  • Fragmented services — movers, cleaners, internet setup sourced informally with no accountability.
  • Diaspora locked out — billions in remittances, almost no reliable way to view, verify, finance, or close on property remotely.
